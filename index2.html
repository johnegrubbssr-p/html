Teams

BB Gunz (BBz)
BB Gunz is a mini level 1 team at the California All-Stars Bay Area. Joe was an original member of BB Gunz during the 2014-15 season. This team competed in the Prep division and won the Prep Grand Champion at the USA Finals. BB Gunz is not currently an active team at Cali.

Young Gunz (Gunz)
Young Gunz is a co-ed youth level 3 team at the California All-Stars Bay Area. Joe was an original member of Young Gunz during the 2015-16 season. Young Gunz is not currently an active team at Cali.

Covert
Covert is a co-ed junior level 4 team at the California All-Stars Bay Area. Joe's sister Annabelle was an original member of Covert during her second season at Cali. Joe was on the first Covert team to compete at The Summit during the 2017-18 season.

Junior Ops (JOps)
Junior Ops is a co-ed junior level 5 team at the California All-Stars Bay Area. This is the first season for Junior Ops, which makes this the third team where Joe has been an original member.
